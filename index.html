About

Kia Ora! My name is Roseanna and I'm a full stack software engineer with over 8 years of experience. I started out my professional career as an iOS and Web Developer, then shifted to Solutions Engineering, and currently my role is Web Solutions Engineer. I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Software Engineering (Hons) at the University of Canterbury (Christchurch, New Zealand). My domain skills include Python, Javascript/Typescript, HTML/CSS, Java, Voice UX, and I enjoy public speaking, teaching and creating.

In my free time I enjoy puzzles, dancing, crafting, reading and playing games (RPGs, MMOs, Roguelikes and Stratgy mainly). Chat to me about cryptic crosswords, upcoming video games, YouTube personalities and your pets.
