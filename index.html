About

Welcome to my corner of the internet. My name is Roseanna and I'm 30 year old software engineer living in London, UK. I've been working in the industry for over 8 years, starting out as a iOS and Web Developer, then shifting to Solutions Engineer, and now back to Software. I'm originally from New Zealand (Kia Ora!) and completed my Bachelor of Engineering (Hons) at the University of Canterbury. My domain skills include Python, Javascript/Typescript, HTML/CSS, Java, Voice UX, and I enjoy public speaking, teaching and creating.

At university I was involved in both the Computing Society, COMPSOC, and the Women in Technology society (now WiTSoc). I tutotored the two 1st year CompSci courses, and 2nd year Algorithms. My Honours thesis was Therapy Personalisation in Software for People With Aphasia in conjunction with the IntelliHealth Research Group. These experiences lead to passions I carry with me: fostering communities, sharing knowledge, explaining and distilling concepts, and creating things to better the world.
